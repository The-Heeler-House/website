A Bluey Discord server? For real life?

Originally started as a small group for r/Place 2022, The Heeler House has grown to become a vibrant community in its own right. Today, over 12,000 people from all around the world are a part of the server. The server is family friendly, and often praised for it's happy vibe and positive feelings.

A wide range of channels exist, from tagbluey-talk, which is a place dedicated to talking about Bluey, to a forumbluey-fanart page, where users showcase their own work for the community to see; A tagbluey-spoilers page where any new information is shared with the world, and tagoff-topic, where you can talk about nearly anything!

Our team of twenty staff, all of which do so for the fun of it, run the server and keep it a safe and vibrant place to be in. If you feel this is the server you want to join, come on in and say G'Day! We'd love to have you join us.

The server rules

The Heeler House has a strict policy on how it's members interacts within the server. In short, the rules are there to help maintain the family-friendly vibe for everyone in the community. For more detailed information on the rules, you may do so through the tagrules channels within the server.

Our moderation team also reserves the right to revoke your access to The Heeler House and associated services for any reason we deem necessary.
